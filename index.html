Still confused about signing up? Check out these questions to clarify your doubts!

How can I customize my meals?

As soon as you login to the Homestylefoods app, it displays all the kitchens near-by along with their menu. You can choose from different kitchens to deliver on different days. Customer ratings & price of the meals make it easier to choose from the many options available.

How is Homestylefoods going to charge me?

We only charge you for the meals you have scheduled & once they have been delivered. You can add funds to your wallet starting from as low as 1000. You can refund the wallet amount as and when you want. Also, Get amazing offers & cashbacks while adding funds to your wallet.

How does Homestylefoods ensure quality?

We have designed an audit with 41 checkpoints for each tiffin delivery service. Our team audits every kitchen once a week. We have multi-check points focusing on Hygiene, Taste, Ingredients, etc. Apart from regular audits, our kitchens have 19 self-audit checkpoints, that they perform everyday before dispatching tiffins!

When can I schedule my meals?

You can plan your meals for the whole month in-advance! We send preparation-orders to the kitchens, 12 hours before the schedule. However, we understand last-minute plans. You can also place an order as before as 2 hours of the scheduled delivery.

Can I cancel/change my scheduled meals?

Yes, Homestylefoods offers this flexibility! You can change your orders for free as before as 3 hours of the scheduled delivery and cancel your order for free as before as 3 hours of the scheduled delivery.

I am a kitchen owner. What is there for me?

Along with customer centric, We are also a kitchen-centric company! There is a lot for you, Join us Now!.
